Required

  • Chapter 2 in Mastering ’Metrics1
  • “Properties of regression” in Causal Inference: The Mixtape2

Recommended

Look through your notes on regression from your last stats class. Also, you can skim through these resources:

  • 6.1–6.4 in ModernDive3
  • 7.1–7.4 in ModernDive4
  • 7.1–7.3 in OpenIntro Statistics5
  • 8.1 in OpenIntro Statistics6

We’ll review all this regression stuff in class, so don’t panic if this all looks terrifying! Also, take advantage of the videos that accompany the OpenIntro chapters. And also, the OpenIntro chapters are heavier on the math—don’t worry if you don’t understand everything.
